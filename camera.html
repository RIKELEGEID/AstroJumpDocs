camera module

class camera.Camera(width, height)[source]

Bases: object

A Camera object that handles the viewing area in the game, focusing on a target entity and limiting the camera’s movement to the dimensions of the map.

Parameters:

  • width – The width of the area the camera can scroll over.

  • height – The height of the area the camera can scroll over.

apply(entity)[source]

Adjusts the position of an entity relative to the current camera position.

Parameters:

entity – The entity whose position will be adjusted for camera offset.

Returns:

The new position of the entity as adjusted by the camera offset.

update(target)[source]

Updates the camera’s position based on the target entity, ensuring the target remains centered in the view, and constraining the camera to the predefined boundaries of the map.

Parameters:

target – The entity the camera should focus on and center in the view.
