Description of Game Mechanics

"AstroJump" follows a set of rules that guide player interactions and progression through the game:

  • Objective: The player's primary goal is to navigate through various levels, avoiding obstacles and reaching the end of each level.
  • Movement: Players can move left or right and jump to navigate platforms and avoid hazards.
  • Collisions: Interacting with enemies or dangerous objects like spikes results in a penalty, specifically losing all progress in the level and getting respawned at the beginning.
  • Levels: Completion of a level is marked by reaching its endpoint, which then unlocks the next level. Failure to complete a level due to falling off the platform or losing all health will prompt a respawn at the beginning.
  • Physics: Gravity affects the player, increasing the velocity with which they fall. This requires players to make precise jumps between platforms.
  • Enemies: Some enemies shoot projectiles that the player must dodge. These projectiles do not collide with the terrain of the level, which results in a very dangerous storm of projectiles.
